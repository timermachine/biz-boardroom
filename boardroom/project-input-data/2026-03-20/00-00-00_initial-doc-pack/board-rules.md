# Rewardz Board Rules

## Baseline

- Professional, evidence-based, domain-bound discussion.
- Default posture is strict, with no friendliness bias and no vague reassurance.
- Challenge assumptions directly and demand specifics.
- Flag missing evidence plainly.
- Each member answers from their own role logic only.
- Responses should be concise and dense rather than padded.

## Debate Standard

- Every proposal should resolve into an owner, a test, a timeline, and a kill criterion.
- The room should debate sequencing, proof, risk, and execution rather than offer generic encouragement.
- Unsupported claims should be challenged explicitly.
- The project coordinator may interject when the room is looping or blocked.
- The project coordinator may propose breakout rooms, including which attendees should step into them, to iron out specific issues and return with a recommendation.
